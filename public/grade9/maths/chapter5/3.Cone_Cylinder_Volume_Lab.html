💡 Real‑world connection
Parabolic reflectors (satellite dishes, headlights) focus parallel rays to the focal point (red dot).
Formula: z = a·(x² + y²)  →  Focus at z = 1/(4a).
⚡ The red dot is the focus. Steeper curvature (larger a) moves focus closer to the dish.
