📐 Scaling Law · 3D Similarity

Scale factor k → Length ∝ k, Area ∝ k², Volume ∝ k³

📐 Length (1D): 1.0 × base
📐 Area (2D): 1.0 × base²
🧊 Volume (3D): 1.0 × base³
