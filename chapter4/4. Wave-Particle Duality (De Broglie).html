De Broglie Duality Lab

λ = h / (mv)
Learning Clues:
- Low mass → Quantum regime: Wave-like behavior dominates, particle is "spread out".
- High mass → Classical regime: Particle behavior dominates, wave effects are negligible.
- Observe how increasing velocity decreases wavelength (λ ∝ 1/v).
- Try combining low mass + high velocity to see short but visible waves.
